Meet the Team with the Vision and Mission to make an Impact

impact The World For Good

Vie is a new sports and entertainment philanthropy company integrating proprietary technology, social competition, collaborative fundraising with matching funds to link Sport with Philanthropy to achieve massive scale and impact the world for good.

Vision

One Technology to deliver a new disruptive B2B and B2C marketplace solution leveraging sport and competition to gamify giving and generate philanthropy at scale.

Mission

To raise billions of dollars for hundreds of thousands of nonprofit organizations worldwide – the causes they impact and the tens of millions of people in need that they serve.

Impact

To  impact player and donor  participation, revenues and donations for benefit of sport and  philanthropy stakeholders worldwide, including charities and foundations; sport associations, leagues, teams, and athletes, sport venues (i.e. , stadiums, golf courses, health clubs), hotels, sponsors, celebrities, influencers and media.

Starting out strong

Vie is is made up of an energetic, experienced team sporting a combined total of over 200 years in the non-profit and sports industries.
