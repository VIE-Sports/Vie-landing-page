How To Vie

Meet the Team with the Vision and Mission to make an Impact

Vie is transforming fundraising into the ultimate competitive sport.

Vie helps NPOs by gamify giving into the ‘Ultimate Competitive Sport’ with leaderboard and prizes for individuals, teams, and NPO Beneficiaries to generate philanthropy at scale.  Vie will help raise donation funds at lower cost and matched to achieve a potential return on individual giving of 100%+.

Vie is a new technology that empowers people

to raise funds for causes worldwide while playing or a fan of the sports they love.

5 Ways to Vie

Benefit

Linking sports with philanthropy allows for multi-charity access of any NPO/NGO to reach the enormous local to global active sports fan base

Access to events NPO/NGOS don't have to originate & operate themselves

Qualifying to Playoffs to Finals event format extends the competition and fundraising period to achieve massive scale play and giving

Challenges offer NPO/NGOS reach to global sports fans making competition out of giving for prizes and benefit of causes

Sparks unlock access to individual users (C2C) through peer-to-peer sharing to play and compete for fun, prizes and charity

"VieMi" - User Avatar - connects with users (Viers) using Al & ML technology connects sport, NPOS/NGOS and tourism stakeholders via competition with Virtual Fundraising Platform
