Say Hi, To Vie

Whether a nonprofit in need of resources, a foundation or corporation seeking a worthy philanthropic outlet, or an individual wanting a new way to play the activities they love with friends near or far- Vie has engineered for all. “A better way to give. A better way to live.”

Marketplace Problem and Vie Competitive Advantage

Nonprofits (‘NPOs’) continuously need money – thus perpetually fundraise

  • New disruptive B2B2C marketplace solution leveraging sport and competition to gamify giving into the ‘Ultimate Competitive Sport’ to raise and distribute billions of dollars to hundreds of thousands of NPOs worldwide.

  • For NPOs turn-key events to increase giving and help raise donations at lower financial and volunteer cost to achieve a potential return on individual giving of 100%+ -- “A better way to give.”

  • For Donors (fans and friends), Vie is the first-ever lifestyle brand to encourage, incentivize and mobilize people worldwide to integrate charitable giving into their everyday sports experiences, workouts and lifestyles. “A better way to live.”

  • For Strategic Partners, Vie delivers significant new revenues and global marketing reach, by engaging/incentivizing the millions of fans/donors - that support them worldwide.

  • For Investors, Vie provides an investment opportunity to “do well, while doing good.”

Vie is transforming fundraising into the ultimate competitive sport.

Vie helps NPOs gamify giving into the ‘Ultimate Competitive Sport’ with leaderboards and prizes for individuals, teams, and NPO beneficiaries to generate giving at scale.  Vie helps NPOs raise donations at a lower cost, which when matched may achieve a return on individual giving of more than 100%.

5 Ways to Vie

Benefits

  • Linking sports with philanthropy allows for any NPO/NGO to reach the enormous local to global sports fan base.

  • NPOs/NGOs gain access to events they don't have to originate & operate themselves.

  • Qualifying to Playoffs to Finals event format extends the competition and fundraising period to achieve massive scale play and giving.

  • Challenges offer NPOs/NGOs reach to pro, club, college and high school sports fans by making competition out of giving to win individual & team prizes.

  • Sparks unlock access to individual users (C2C) through peer-to-peer sharing to play and compete for fun, prizes and charity.

  • "VieMi" - User Avatar – connects users (Viers) using Al & ML technology via competition with Vie Virtual Fundraising Platform.
